YOUR ADELAIDEAN TEAM

Developing and building landmark residential and commercial high-rises in Adelaide for over two decades, Kyren Group has become a highly respected development corporation. Incorporating the latest in sustainable technology with state-of-the-art design, our high-profile projects consistently set new benchmarks.

Projected specialises in sourcing and creating development opportunities and delivering them to investors and developers in a strong and saleable position. They work closely with the entire development team to ensure projects are successful – from architects to service engineers, builders, project and development managers. Projected bridge the gap between agents and developers to ensure successful outcomes.

Brown Falconer is a local Adelaide based, multidisciplinary design practice boasting architecture, interior design and building services engineering expertise across a range of development sectors. The business prides itself on being one of the longest-standing architectural practices in South Australia.
