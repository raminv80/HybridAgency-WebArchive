Energy to be more, every day.

Kanguru’s Daily blend includes 13 tailored ingredients, with five unique, natural botanicals, each scientifically chosen based on best evidence to bring real energy and real benefits for the thinker as much as the doer.

The result? A synergistic, multifunctional sustenance beverage filled with natural benefits. As an anti-crash sugar-free energy drink, Kanguru gives you the energy and benefits to be more, every day. That means a smoother lift – because everyday life is a marathon, not just a sprint.

Kanguru benefits are substantial, a few of which are:

  • Scientifically developed sustenance
  • 0% sugar content only
  • Anti-crash by design
  • 5 key best-quality super-botanicals unique to Kanguru, and in active amounts, creating a Yerba Mate, Guarana, Korean Red Ginseng, Schisandra and Green Tea-driven energy drink
  • Caffeine, over 90% of which is delivered through our botanicals rather than simply being added, and at lesser amounts than a café coffee
  • Taurine and 6 key B-vitamins
  • A multifunctional evidence-based formula designed for best effect with two 250ml cans per day
  • A refreshing, enjoyable taste

Kanguru is a member of the Australian Beverages Council.
